# Horse Powered

Welcome to Horse Powered! This mod adds a variety of horse-powered machinery for processing items without requiring electricity or fuel - just the power of your trusty steed.

## Features

- **Manual Processing**: Use the Hand Grindstone and Chopping Block to process items by hand
- **Automated Processing**: Attach a horse or other creature to automate your grindstone, chopper, or press
- **Multiple Recipes**: Grind wheat into flour, chop logs into planks, and press items into liquids

## Getting Started

Start by crafting a [Hand Grindstone](hand_grindstone.md) for manual grinding, or jump straight into automation with the [Horse Grindstone](horse_grindstone.md).

## Blocks

### Manual Machines
- [Hand Grindstone](hand_grindstone.md) - Grind items by hand
- [Chopping Block](chopping_block.md) - Chop logs with an axe

### Horse-Powered Machines
- [Horse Grindstone](horse_grindstone.md) - Automated grinding powered by a horse
- [Horse Chopper](horse_chopper.md) - Automated chopping powered by a horse
- [Horse Press](horse_press.md) - Press items into fluids or other products

## Tips

- Horses and other creatures can be attached to horse-powered blocks by leading them with a lead and right-clicking the block
- Horse-powered blocks require a clear 7x7 area around them for the animal to walk
- Shift+Right-click a horse-powered block with an empty hand to see its required working area
